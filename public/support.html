Quick Answers

How do I request a refund?

Email us at support@drift-global.com with your trip details and reason for the refund request. We'll review and respond within 24-48 hours.

I left something in the car. What do I do?

Contact us immediately at support@drift-global.com with your trip details. We'll help connect you with the driver to retrieve your item.

How do I update my payment method?

Open the Drift app, go to Settings, then Payment Methods. You can add, remove, or update your cards there.

How do I become a driver?

Download the Drift app, sign up as a driver, and complete the registration process including document verification. We'll review your application and get back to you.
