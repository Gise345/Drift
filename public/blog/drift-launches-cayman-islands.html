Drift Launches: Cayman Islands Gets Its First Modern Carpooling Platform

DG
Drift Global
Official Announcement
December 20, 2024
5 min read

GEORGE TOWN, CAYMAN ISLANDS — Today marks a new chapter in transportation for the Cayman Islands. We're thrilled to announce the official launch of Drift, the first peer-to-peer carpooling platform designed specifically for our island community.

For years, residents and tourists alike have faced a common challenge: getting around Grand Cayman efficiently and affordably. While the island's natural beauty is unmatched, transportation options have remained limited. Traditional taxis can be expensive and hard to find, especially during peak tourist seasons or late-night hours. That's exactly why we built Drift.

Built by Caymanians, for Everyone

Drift isn't just another app — it's a movement. We're proud to be 100% locally owned and operated. Our team understands the unique needs of island life because we live it every day. From navigating the busy streets of George Town during cruise ship arrivals to ensuring safe rides home after a night at Seven Mile Beach, we've thought of everything.

Our platform connects riders with verified local drivers who know every corner of Grand Cayman. Whether you need a quick ride to the grocery store, a trip from Owen Roberts International Airport to your hotel, or transportation to explore the island's hidden gems, Drift has you covered.

How Drift Works

Getting started with Drift is simple:

  1. Download the App — Available now on Google Play, with iOS coming soon
  2. Create Your Account — Sign up in under 2 minutes with your phone number
  3. Enter Your Destination — Tell us where you want to go
  4. See Your Price — Transparent, upfront pricing with no hidden fees
  5. Get Matched — A verified driver accepts your ride within minutes
  6. Track and Ride — Watch your driver arrive in real-time, then enjoy your trip

What Makes Drift Different

We've built Drift with the community's needs at heart. Here's what sets us apart:

Transparent Pricing

No more negotiating fares or wondering what you'll pay. With Drift, you see the exact price before you book. Our rates are competitive and fair, benefiting both riders and drivers.

Verified Local Drivers

Every Drift driver undergoes a thorough background check and verification process. They're your neighbors, friends, and fellow Caymanians who take pride in providing safe, friendly service.

Cashless Convenience

Pay securely through the app with your credit or debit card. No cash needed, no awkward payment moments at the end of your ride.

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Know exactly when your driver will arrive and share your trip details with friends or family for added peace of mind.

24/7 Availability

Need a ride at 3 AM? Drift is available around the clock, every day of the year. Night owls and early birds alike can count on us.

"We started Drift because we saw an opportunity to make transportation in the Cayman Islands better for everyone. Our goal is simple: connect people who need rides with people who can provide them, safely and affordably."

For Riders: Your Island, Your Way

Whether you're a tourist exploring Grand Cayman for the first time or a local heading to work, Drift makes getting around effortless. Popular routes include:

  • Airport Transfers — From Owen Roberts International to anywhere on the island
  • Seven Mile Beach — Hotels, restaurants, and beach clubs
  • George Town — Shopping, dining, and the cruise port
  • Stingray City — Tour pickups and drop-offs
  • Rum Point — Beach outings and day trips
  • East End — Resorts and local communities

For Drivers: Earn on Your Schedule

Drift isn't just for riders — it's an opportunity for Cayman residents to earn extra income on their own terms. As a Drift driver, you'll enjoy:

  • Flexible Hours — Drive when you want, as much as you want
  • Weekly Payouts — Get paid directly to your account every week
  • Keep More Earnings — Competitive driver rates that put money in your pocket
  • 24/7 Support — Our team is always here to help

Ready to start driving? Download the Drift app and complete your driver registration. We're actively onboarding drivers across Grand Cayman.

Looking Ahead

This launch is just the beginning. In the coming months, we'll be rolling out new features including:

  • iOS app release for iPhone users
  • Scheduled rides for airport pickups
  • Group rides and ride-sharing options
  • Loyalty rewards for frequent riders
  • Enhanced driver benefits and incentives

We're committed to listening to our community and continuously improving the Drift experience. Your feedback matters — reach out to us anytime at support@drift-global.com.

Thank You, Cayman

To everyone who has supported us on this journey — thank you. From our early beta testers to our first drivers, from our families to our friends who believed in this vision — Drift is for you.

Together, we're not just building an app. We're building a community. A movement. A better way to get around the place we all call home.
