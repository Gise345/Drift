How Drift Keeps You Safe: Our Commitment to Rider and Driver Security

DG
Drift Global
Safety Team
December 8, 2024
5 min read

At Drift, safety isn't just a feature — it's the foundation of everything we build. Whether you're a rider exploring Grand Cayman or a driver earning income on your schedule, your wellbeing is our top priority. Here's a comprehensive look at how we keep our community safe.

For Riders: Peace of Mind on Every Trip

Verified Drivers

Every Drift driver goes through a thorough vetting process before they can accept rides:

  • Background checks: We verify criminal history and driving records
  • Identity verification: Drivers must provide valid documentation and photos
  • Vehicle inspection: Cars must meet our safety and comfort standards
  • Insurance verification: All vehicles must have valid insurance

Before you get in any Drift vehicle, you can see your driver's name, photo, and rating — so you know exactly who's picking you up.

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Every Drift ride is tracked from pickup to dropoff. This means:

  • You can watch your driver approach on the map before pickup
  • During the ride, your route is monitored in real-time
  • Trip records are stored for reference if needed
  • Any unusual route deviations can be flagged

Share Your Trip

Let friends and family know you're on your way. With one tap, you can share your trip details including:

  • Driver's name and photo
  • Vehicle details
  • Real-time location during the trip
  • Estimated arrival time

Your loved ones can watch your journey and know exactly when you'll arrive safely.

Cashless Payments

No cash means no risk. All payments are processed securely through the app, eliminating:

  • The need to carry cash
  • Disputes over fares
  • Awkward payment moments at the end of rides

Your payment information is encrypted and protected with industry-standard security.

Ratings and Reviews

Our two-way rating system keeps everyone accountable:

  • Rate your driver after each trip
  • Read reviews from other riders before booking
  • Drivers with consistently low ratings are removed from the platform

This community-driven approach ensures quality and safety for everyone.

For Drivers: Protection on the Road

Verified Riders

Just as riders can trust our drivers, drivers can trust our riders:

  • All riders must verify their phone numbers
  • Payment methods are validated before booking
  • Rider ratings help drivers make informed decisions

Trip Transparency

Drivers see important information before accepting any ride:

  • Rider's name and rating
  • Pickup location
  • Destination (when provided)
  • Estimated fare

24/7 Support

Our local support team is always available. If something doesn't feel right, drivers can:

  • Contact support directly through the app
  • Report concerns about specific riders
  • Get help with any issues in real-time

Driver Ratings of Riders

Drivers rate riders after each trip. Riders who receive consistently poor ratings may be removed from the platform, creating a safer environment for all drivers.

Emergency Features

In-App Emergency Button

If you ever feel unsafe during a ride, our emergency features are just a tap away. The app provides quick access to:

  • Local emergency services
  • Drift support team
  • Share location with trusted contacts

24/7 Customer Support

Our dedicated support team operates around the clock. Whether it's 3 PM or 3 AM, help is always available. Reach us through:

  • In-app support chat
  • Email: support@drift-global.com
  • Direct phone support for urgent issues

COVID-19 Safety Measures

While the pandemic situation has improved, we maintain health and safety best practices:

  • Drivers are encouraged to keep vehicles well-ventilated
  • Regular vehicle cleaning is part of our driver guidelines
  • Riders and drivers can wear masks if they choose

Our Safety Commitment

Safety is an ongoing journey, not a destination. We continuously work to improve our safety features by:

  • Listening to feedback: Rider and driver reports help us identify areas for improvement
  • Updating technology: We regularly enhance our app's safety features
  • Training drivers: Ongoing education on safety best practices
  • Working with local authorities: Collaborating with local law enforcement when needed
"As a small island community, trust matters. When you get in a Drift car, you're not getting in with a stranger — you're riding with a neighbor. We take that responsibility seriously."

Tips for a Safe Ride

For Riders

  • Verify your driver and vehicle match the app details before getting in
  • Share your trip with a friend or family member
  • Sit in the back seat if traveling alone
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels off, don't take the ride
  • Use the app for all communication and payment

For Drivers

  • Verify your rider's name before starting the trip
  • Keep your vehicle clean and well-maintained
  • Follow the app's suggested route
  • Report any concerns immediately
  • Don't share personal contact information with riders

Your Safety is Our Priority

At Drift, we've built safety into every aspect of our platform. From verified drivers and real-time tracking to 24/7 support, we're committed to making every ride as safe as possible.

  • In-App: Use the Help section in the Drift app
