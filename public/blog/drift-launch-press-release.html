Drift Launches as Cayman Islands' First Peer-to-Peer Carpooling Platform, Offering Safe and Affordable Transportation Alternative

Local startup bridges critical public transit gap with innovative carpooling app, already featured in Cayman Compass

DG
Drift Global
Official Press Release
December 26, 2025
6 min read

GEORGE TOWN, GRAND CAYMAN — Drift, a locally-founded technology company, has officially launched as the Cayman Islands' first peer-to-peer carpooling platform, offering residents and visitors a safe, affordable, and legal alternative to traditional transportation in a market where global rideshare giants Uber and Lyft are not permitted to operate.

The carpooling app, which operates under the peer-to-peer model rather than commercial rideshare regulations, connects riders with verified local drivers across Grand Cayman. Available 24/7, Drift provides upfront transparent pricing, cashless in-app payments, real-time GPS tracking, and robust safety features—expectations that have become standard for modern travelers worldwide.

"Drift isn't just an app—it's a movement. We're creating economic opportunities for local drivers while solving a real transportation problem for our community and the millions of tourists who visit our beautiful islands each year." — Drift Global Team

Addressing a Critical Infrastructure Need

The Cayman Islands, a premier Caribbean tourism destination welcoming over 2.4 million visitors annually, has long faced transportation challenges. The government's ban on traditional rideshare services like Uber and Lyft—implemented to protect the local taxi industry—has left a significant void in the market. Taxi fares can be unpredictable and expensive, while public bus coverage remains limited, operating primarily during daytime hours.

Drift's peer-to-peer carpooling model offers a legal alternative that fills this gap. The platform has already garnered significant local media attention, with the Cayman Compass, the island's leading news publication, featuring the company in a December 2025 investigation titled "Carpool Apps Test the Limits of the Law to Fill Public Transport Gaps."

Key Features That Set Drift Apart

The Drift platform differentiates itself through several rider-focused features:

  • Upfront Pricing — Riders see exact fares before booking with no surprises or hidden fees
  • Verified Local Drivers — All drivers undergo a document verification process to ensure legitimacy
  • Cashless Payments — Secure credit card payments through the app eliminate the need for cash
  • Real-Time GPS Tracking — Riders can track their driver's arrival and share trip details with family and friends
  • Island-Wide Coverage — Service spans all major areas including Seven Mile Beach, George Town, Owen Roberts International Airport, Stingray City, Rum Point, East End, West Bay, and Bodden Town
  • 24/7 Availability — Round-the-clock service meets the needs of early morning airport transfers and late-night outings alike

Meeting Tourism Demand

The launch arrives at a pivotal moment for Caribbean tourism. With the global ride-sharing market projected to reach $185 billion by 2026 according to industry analysts, travelers increasingly expect seamless, app-based transportation regardless of destination. Drift positions the Cayman Islands to meet these modern expectations while maintaining the local character that makes the destination unique.

"Modern travelers expect modern solutions. With real-time GPS tracking, upfront pricing, and cashless payments, Drift delivers the transportation experience people are accustomed to worldwide, with the personal touch that only local drivers can provide." — Drift Global Team

Driving Economic Opportunity

Beyond serving riders, Drift creates flexible earning opportunities for Cayman residents. Drivers set their own schedules, receive weekly payouts, and benefit from dedicated support team. The platform's competitive commission structure ensures drivers retain more of their earnings compared to traditional taxi arrangements.

Benefits for Drivers:

  • Flexible Hours — Drive when you want, as much as you want
  • Weekly Payouts — Get paid directly to your account every week
  • Keep More Earnings — Competitive driver rates that put money in your pocket
  • Support — Dedicated team always available to help

Popular Routes in Grand Cayman

Drift provides convenient transportation across all of Grand Cayman's most popular destinations:

  • Airport Transfers — Owen Roberts International Airport to anywhere on the island
  • Seven Mile Beach — Hotels, resorts, restaurants, and beach clubs
  • George Town — Shopping, dining, cruise port, and downtown
  • Stingray City — Tour pickups and drop-offs
  • Rum Point — Beach outings and day trips
  • East End — Resorts, beaches, and local communities
  • West Bay — Turtle Centre and local attractions
  • Bodden Town — Historic sites and residential areas

Availability and Access

Drift is currently available for download on Google Play for Android devices, with an iOS version for Apple devices coming soon. Both tourists and residents can sign up as riders instantly, while prospective drivers can apply through the app to join the verified driver network.
