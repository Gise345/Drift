How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your Drift account, you can do so directly within the app. Follow the steps below based on whether you're using Drift as a rider or driver.

1

Open the Drift App

Launch the Drift app on your mobile device and sign in to your account.

2

Go to Settings

For Riders: Tap the menu icon and select "Settings".
For Drivers: Tap the menu icon, then select "Settings" from the menu.

3

Find Account Section

Scroll down to the "Account" section in the Settings menu.

4

Tap "Delete Account"

Select the "Delete Account" option. You will be asked to confirm your decision twice to prevent accidental deletion.

5

Confirm Deletion

After confirming, your account will be immediately deactivated and you will be signed out.

Important: Account deletion is permanent. Once deleted, you will not be able to access your account, trip history, or any associated data. You would need to create a new account to use Drift again.

What Data is Deleted vs. Retained

When you delete your account, we handle your data as follows:

Data Type What Happens Status
Login Credentials Permanently deleted - you cannot sign in again Deleted
Profile Information Marked as deleted, access revoked Deleted
Payment Methods Removed from your account Deleted
Trip History Retained for 7 years for legal/tax compliance Retained
Transaction Records Retained for 7 years for financial/legal compliance Retained
Driver Documents Retained for 3 years for regulatory compliance Retained
Why do we retain some data?
Certain data must be retained to comply with Cayman Islands financial regulations, tax requirements, and to resolve any potential disputes. This data is securely stored and not used for any other purpose.

Request Data Deletion Without Deleting Account

If you would like to request deletion of specific data while keeping your account active, you can contact us directly. We can delete:

  • Profile photo
  • Saved addresses
  • Saved payment methods
  • Notification preferences

Note: Some data (like trip history and transaction records) cannot be deleted while maintaining an active account due to our operational and legal requirements.

Automatic Data Deletion

Drift automatically deletes certain data over time:

  • Location data: Trip location data is anonymized after 90 days
  • Chat messages: In-app messages are deleted 30 days after trip completion
  • Search history: Automatically cleared after 30 days

Need Help?

If you're having trouble deleting your account through the app, or if you have questions about your data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@drift-global.com
  • Privacy inquiries: privacy@drift-global.com

Please include your registered email address in your request so we can locate your account. We will respond within 5 business days.
