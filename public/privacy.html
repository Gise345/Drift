Drift Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Introduction

Drift in Cayman Ltd. ("Drift," "we," "our," or "us") operates a peer-to-peer community technology platform that connects independent individuals who wish to share private carpool arrangements within the Cayman Islands. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Important: Drift is a technology platform, NOT a transportation company. We do not own vehicles, employ drivers, or provide transportation services. All carpool arrangements are private, voluntary agreements between independent users.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Date of birth (to verify minimum age: 16+ for riders, 21+ for drivers)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe)

Driver Information

If you register as a driver (must be 21+), we additionally collect:

  • Valid Cayman Islands driver's license
  • Vehicle registration and insurance documentation
  • Background check information
  • Bank account details for receiving cost-sharing contributions

Location Data Collection

Important: Location data is central to how Drift connects users for private carpool arrangements. Please read this section carefully.

Foreground Location

When the app is open, we collect your location to:

  • Show nearby available drivers on the map
  • Calculate suggested routes and cost-sharing contributions
  • Provide turn-by-turn navigation during trips
  • Match you with drivers based on GPS proximity

Background Location

During active trips only, we collect location data even when the app is in the background or your screen is off. This enables:

  • Trip Safety Sharing: Share your real-time location with trusted contacts (friends, family) who can monitor your trip via a web link
  • Accurate ETAs: Provide accurate arrival time updates to other users
  • Navigation: Allow drivers to receive turn-by-turn directions even with the screen off
  • Trip Records: Record trip routes for safety and dispute resolution

When Location Collection Stops

Background location collection automatically stops when:

  • A trip is completed or cancelled
  • A driver goes offline
  • You log out of the app

Your Control Over Location Data

You can disable background location access in your device settings at any time. However, this may limit safety features like trip sharing. Foreground location is required for core app functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Facilitate connections between users for private carpool arrangements
  • Process cost-sharing contributions through our secure payment system
  • Communicate with you about trips and account updates
  • Provide safety features including trip tracking and SOS functionality
  • Investigate and resolve disputes between users
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Improve our platform and services

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information or location data to advertisers or data brokers.

We may share your information with:

  • Other Users: Riders see driver name, photo, vehicle info, rating, and real-time location during trips. Drivers see rider name, photo, rating, and pickup/destination.
  • Contacts You Choose: When you share a trip, selected contacts can view your real-time location via a secure web link.
  • Service Providers: Companies that help us operate (payment processing via Stripe, cloud hosting via Firebase/Google Cloud, mapping services) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Law Enforcement: When required by valid legal process (subpoena, court order) under Cayman Islands law.
  • Safety Emergencies: If we believe disclosure is necessary to prevent harm.

Cost-Sharing Payments

For the safety and protection of all users, Drift requires all cost-sharing contributions to be processed through our secure in-app payment system. This policy:

  • Protects drivers from non-payment disputes
  • Protects riders from price changes after agreement
  • Eliminates cash-related safety risks
  • Provides documented transaction records for dispute resolution

A 19% platform service fee is applied to cover payment processing (~4%) and platform maintenance costs (~15%).

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (Google Cloud/Firebase)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • PCI compliance for payment processing
  • Regular security assessments

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Trip history is retained for safety, dispute resolution, and legal purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Age Requirements

  • Riders: Must be at least 16 years old (legal age in Cayman Islands)
  • Drivers: Must be at least 21 years old with a valid Cayman Islands driver's license

We do not knowingly collect information from users under 16 years of age.

Your Rights

Under Cayman Islands Data Protection Act and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing
  • Data portability

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email. Continued use of Drift after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
