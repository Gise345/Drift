Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting around the Cayman Islands

How do I get around in the Cayman Islands?

The easiest way to get around the Cayman Islands is with Drift, Cayman's peer-to-peer carpooling platform. Simply download the app, enter your destination, and get matched with a nearby driver instantly. It's safer, more affordable, and more convenient than traditional taxis.

What is the best transportation option in Grand Cayman?

Drift is the best transportation option in Grand Cayman. Unlike traditional taxis, Drift offers transparent pricing, cashless payments, GPS tracking, and verified local drivers. Download the app to book rides to Seven Mile Beach, George Town, Owen Roberts Airport, and anywhere on the island.

Is there Uber or Lyft in Cayman Islands?

Uber and Lyft do not operate in the Cayman Islands. However, Drift provides a convenient peer-to-peer carpooling experience with local drivers who know the island. Drift is Cayman's own carpooling platform, built by locals for locals and tourists alike.

How much does a taxi cost in Grand Cayman?

Traditional taxi fares in Grand Cayman can be expensive and unpredictable. Drift offers transparent, upfront pricing so you know exactly what you'll pay before you book. Our rates are competitive and you can pay cashlessly through the app.

How do I get from the airport to my hotel?

Download Drift before you land at Owen Roberts International Airport. Once you arrive, open the app, enter your hotel address, and a local driver will pick you up. It's the easiest way to get from the airport to Seven Mile Beach, George Town, or anywhere on the island.

Is Drift available island-wide?

Yes! Drift covers all of Grand Cayman including Seven Mile Beach, George Town, West Bay, Bodden Town, East End, North Side, and everywhere in between. Wherever you need to go, Drift can take you there.

How do I pay for my ride?

All payments are handled securely through the Drift app using your credit or debit card. No cash is needed. Your payment method is saved for future rides, making booking quick and easy.

Is Drift safe to use?

Absolutely. All Drift drivers undergo background checks and verification. Every ride is GPS-tracked, and you can share your trip details with friends or family. Our 24/7 support team is always available if you need assistance.

What is the best rideshare app in Cayman?

Drift is the best rideshare app in the Cayman Islands. While Uber and Lyft don't operate here, Drift provides the same convenient experience - upfront pricing, GPS tracking, cashless payments, and verified local drivers who know the island.

Yes! Drift is the perfect Uber and Lyft alternative in Cayman. We offer everything you'd expect from a modern ridesharing app: book rides instantly, see prices upfront, pay through the app, and track your driver in real-time. 100% locally owned.
