4. How Drift Works

4.1 The Three-Party Structure:

  • Riders: Independent individuals seeking carpool arrangements who make voluntary requests and choose which drivers to connect with
  • Drivers: Independent individuals offering to share rides who voluntarily accept or decline requests. Drivers are NOT employees, contractors, or agents of Drift
  • Drift: Provides technology infrastructure only and does NOT participate in the actual carpool arrangement

4.2 Connection Process:

  1. Rider enters pickup location and destination
  2. Platform suggests nearby available drivers based on GPS proximity
  3. Rider selects preferred driver from available options
  4. Driver receives notification and voluntarily chooses to accept or decline
  5. If both parties agree, they connect for a private carpool arrangement
  6. Private carpool takes place (arranged between users, not by Drift)
  7. Cost-sharing payment processed through the app
Important: At NO point does Drift dispatch, assign, mandate, or control any aspect of the ride itself. All arrangements are voluntary, private agreements between independent users.

5. Cost-Sharing & Payments

5.1 Suggested Contributions:

Drift provides SUGGESTED cost contribution ranges based on distance, time, and fuel costs. These are suggestions only—not mandatory fares. Final amounts are voluntary agreements between driver and rider.

5.2 Platform Service Fee:

A 19% platform service fee is applied to all cost-sharing contributions processed through the app:

  • ~4% covers payment processing (Stripe fees, transaction security, PCI compliance)
  • ~15% covers platform maintenance (servers, APIs, engineering, support, safety features)

5.3 Mandatory In-App Payment:

For the safety and protection of all users, ALL cost-sharing payments must be processed through the in-app payment system. This protects:

  • Drivers from: Non-payment, cash disputes, handling cash risks
  • Riders from: Price changes mid-ride, payment disputes, cash handling risks

Cash payments are not permitted and may result in account suspension.
